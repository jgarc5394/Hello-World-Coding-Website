Celebrating Our Students!

Here at Hello World! Coding, we take a lot of pride in our work and our students. We like to celebrate the success of our students and share their success with the community. Every month we choose a student's project to showcase on our website. This month, we have chosen Julio Garcia as our Star Student with his program named "Coffee Cafe."

"Coffee Cafe" is a dynamic menu driven program that utilizes class objects of Node pointers with queues to simulate a Coffee Cafe ordering system. It presents a menu to the user, who has the ability to "Take an Order, Close out an Order, and List all pending Orders" in the system. As an order is taken, the order is stored in the class object attributes of an OrderNode, and when that order is completed, the OrderNode is added to the OrderQueue. Each time an order is closed out, the first order in the queue is removed. This program also showcases how to catch Underflow Exceptions!
